About

Code is architecture.
We treat it that way.

NEON is a digital systems studio. We design and engineer backends, APIs, data pipelines, and interfaces for teams who need things to work at scale — reliably, securely, and without ceremony.

Founded by engineers who got tired of fragile systems dressed up in pretty dashboards. We focus on what matters: correctness, performance, and maintainability.
