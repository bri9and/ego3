How It Works

Up and running in minutes

No code, no complex setup. Just results.

01

Make Your Ego

Create your AI voice agent. Pick a voice, set the personality, define what it knows about your business, and tell it how to handle calls. Your Ego is built to sound like you — not a robot.

02

Assign a Number

Get a new local or toll-free number, or forward your existing line. Your Ego picks up every call on the first ring — no hold music, no voicemail, no missed opportunities.

03

Create a Job

Pair your Ego with a number and go live. For inbound, it starts answering immediately. For outbound, upload a contact list, set your script, and launch a campaign that reaches hundreds in minutes.

04

Monitor & Optimize

Every call is logged with a full transcript and AI summary. Review outcomes, see who called, what they needed, and how it was handled. Refine your Ego's responses and watch performance improve over time.
